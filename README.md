## 🚀 Case Studies

### HospitalityIQ — Revenue Intelligence for Hotels
Problem: Hotel revenue managers spend 4+ hours/day in spreadsheets, missing real-time signals.
What I built: An AI analytics layer that surfaces occupancy anomalies and pricing opportunities.
PM Lens: Discovery → PRD → Prototype → Validation

### CS Automation — From Manual to Scalable
Problem: CS teams at B2B SaaS companies lack early warning systems for churn risk.
What I built: A health scoring engine plus automated playbook triggers.
PM Lens: Pain → Hypothesis → Build → Measure
